Her work has won a best paper runner-up at ICML 2022, a best paper award at IISA 2018, and a best paper award at USENIX LISA 2014. His research focuses on responsibly building AI systems and draws on ideas from signal processing and applied mathematics. His work at the boundary between theory and systems, leveraging mathematical foundations and efficient hardware to improve the training, fine-tuning, benchmarking, and robustness of large models. He has been the recipient of several awards, including SIAM's DiPrima Prize, a DARPA Young Faculty Award, and a Sloan Fellowship. Tom has done influential research on recurrent networks and inference-time scaling for latent thinking with them.</td>
